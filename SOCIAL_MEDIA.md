## Reddit (r/Amplenote & r/Productivity)
**[Release] The ultimate Timestamp Plugin (Analog Clocks, Natural Text, UTC, and more!)**

Hey everyone!

I just released v0.0.20 of a new **Timestamp Plugin** for Amplenote. I wanted a single tool that could handle *any* kind of date/time insertion without needing multiple plugins, so I built this as a "Swiss Army Knife" for time.

**What it does:**
- `{ Digital }`: Insert completely custom date strings (supports Day.js tokens and UTC).
- `{ Analog }`: Believe it or not, this generates a real visual SVG clock face showing the current time and attaches it as an image directly into your note. It supports Dark, Light, and Neon themes!
- `{ Roman }`: For the history buffs, it outputs your current date/time entirely in Roman numerals.
- `{ Text }`: Outputs natural conversational time (e.g., "It's quarter to Two").
- **Unix Replacer**: Highlight a Unix timestamp in your note, run the command, and it replaces it with your local timezone equivalent.

**Why I built it:**
I often work across timezones and occasionally need to insert quick Unix-to-local conversions while debugging logs in my notes. I also just really wanted a cool analog clock in my daily jots. The plugin is heavily tested, performance-optimized, and gracefully handles edge cases (no crashes!).
