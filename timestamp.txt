3. Type `/Timestamp: Analog`, and hit Enter/Return > Check the Output.
4. Type `/Timestamp: Test`, and hit Enter/Return > Check the Output.
5. Type `/Timestamp: Unix`, and hit Enter/Return > Check the Output.
